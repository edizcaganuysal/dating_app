================================================================================
                    YUNI — COMPLETE APP FLOW
================================================================================

    This document describes every user touchpoint, screen, notification,
    and decision point in the Yuni app — incorporating all research-based
    redesign recommendations.

    Legend:
    [SCREEN]     = A visible app screen
    (ACTION)     = User action
    {SYSTEM}     = Backend/algorithm action (invisible to user)
    >>NOTIFY>>   = Push notification
    --IF--       = Conditional branch
    **NEW**      = New feature from research redesign
    **CHANGED**  = Modified from current implementation
    ~FEMALE~     = Female-first design element

PHASE 1: ONBOARDING (Single Path, ~5 minutes)
================================================================================

**CHANGED** Merged quick/thorough into one optimized path.
Every question backed by research evidence.

1.1  [PHOTOS SCREEN]
     "Add 3-6 photos of yourself"
     - Photo grid (3 minimum, 6 maximum)
     - Aspect ratio 4:5
     - Drag to reorder
     (Upload from camera roll or take new)
     {SYSTEM} ML attractiveness scoring on upload (invisible)
     (Tap: Next)
     --IF-- < 3 photos: "Add at least 3 photos to continue"

1.2  [SELFIE VERIFICATION SCREEN]
     "Take a quick selfie to verify it's really you"
     - Live camera view with face outline
     - Instructions: "Look straight at the camera"
     (Take selfie)
     {SYSTEM} Compare to uploaded photos
     {SYSTEM} Set selfie_status = "pending" (admin reviews)
     (Tap: Next)

1.3  [BASICS SCREEN]
     Auto-populated where possible:
     - Age: computed from birthdate (display only)
     - University: auto-detected from email domain
     - Location: "Allow location access" prompt
       --IF-- Denied: manual address entry with autocomplete
     (Tap: Next)

1.4  [WHAT YOU'RE LOOKING FOR SCREEN] **CHANGED**
     "What are you looking for?"
     - Relationship intent: Casual / Serious / Open to either
       (single select, large tappable cards)
     - Age range: dual slider (min/max, default 18-25)
     - Dealbreakers (max 3): **CHANGED** Capped at 3
       [Smoking, Heavy drinking, Different religion,
        Long distance, Rude to service staff]
       (multi-select chips, counter shows "X/3 selected")
     (Tap: Next)

1.5  [YOUR VALUES SCREEN] **NEW**
     "Quick round — which matters more to you?"
     6 binary forced-choice cards, swipe or tap:
     (a) Ambition ←→ Work-life balance
     (b) Tradition ←→ Open-mindedness
     (c) Independence ←→ Togetherness
     (d) Adventure ←→ Stability
     (e) Spontaneity ←→ Planning
     (f) Brutal honesty ←→ Kind diplomacy
     Card flip animation for each choice. Fast, engaging.
     {SYSTEM} Stores as 6-bit binary values_vector
     (Auto-advances after 6th choice)

1.6  [YOUR VIBE IN GROUPS SCREEN] **CHANGED**
     "How do you show up in groups?"
     - Social energy: 1-5 slider with emoji faces
       (1 = "Quiet observer" → 5 = "Life of the party")
     - Group role: single select (was multi-select)
       "In a group, I'm usually the one who..."
       (a) Gets conversation started [Catalyst]
       (b) Makes everyone laugh [Entertainer]
       (c) Listens and asks great questions [Listener]
       (d) Plans and organizes everything [Planner]
       (e) Goes with the flow [Flexible]
     (Tap: Next)

1.7  [ACTIVITIES & INTERESTS SCREEN] **CHANGED**
     "What would you do on a group date?"
     - Activity preferences: select 3+ from curated list
       (escape room, cooking class, trivia, hiking, karaoke,
        bowling, board games, dinner, bar, mini golf, etc.)
       **NEW** Activities sorted by predicted conversion rate
       **NEW** Social proof: "Most popular: Escape Rooms 🔥"
     - Interests: select up to 10 from ~40 items **CHANGED**
       (was 110+ items, now streamlined)
       Categories: Sports, Music, Creative, Food, Entertainment,
                   Outdoors, Social, Intellectual
     (Tap: Next)

1.8  [PROMPTS SCREEN]
     "Choose 2 prompts and fill them in"
     - Scroll through 10 prompt options
     - Tap to select, then choose from pre-made answers or write custom
     - Must complete exactly 2
     (Tap: Done → Profile review screen)

1.9  [PROFILE REVIEW SCREEN]
     Shows complete profile as others will see it:
     - Photos, name, age, university
     - Prompts, interests
     - Relationship intent (public)
     - NOT shown: dealbreakers, values, age range (private)
     (Tap: Looks good! → Home screen)
     (Tap: Edit → back to relevant step)

     {SYSTEM} Profile marked as complete
     {SYSTEM} User eligible for matching

PHASE 3: MATCHING (Backend)
================================================================================

3.1  {SYSTEM} Batch matching runs daily at set time (e.g., 8pm)
     - Hard constraint filtering (Section 6.2 of research)
     - Attractiveness band clustering
     - Group-level Q scoring (NOT pairwise compatibility)
     - Epsilon-greedy: 15% random groups for exploration
     - Greedy assignment with 2-opt local search, 20 restarts

3.2  {SYSTEM} Groups formed, assigned activity + time
     {SYSTEM} Create DateGroup record
     {SYSTEM} Create GroupMember records
     {SYSTEM} Create group ChatRoom

3.3  **NEW** ~FEMALE~ Women confirm first:
     >>NOTIFY>> to women in group:
     "Your group date is set! 🎉
      [Activity] on [Day] at [Time]
      Tap to see your group and confirm."

3.4  [GROUP REVEAL SCREEN — WOMEN]
     Shows all group members: name, photos, age, university
     Shows activity, date, time
     (Tap: I'm in! → Confirm)
     (Tap: I can't make it → Cancel flow)
     --IF-- Cancel: {SYSTEM} attempts replacement from pool
            If no replacement found, remaining group votes

3.5  --IF-- All women confirmed:
     >>NOTIFY>> to men in group:
     "Your group date is set! 🎉
      [Activity] on [Day] at [Time]
      Tap to see your group and confirm."

3.6  [GROUP REVEAL SCREEN — MEN]
     Same as women's screen.
     (Tap: I'm in! → Confirm)
     (Tap: I can't make it → Cancel flow)

3.7  {SYSTEM} All confirmed → Group chat opens
     {SYSTEM} Schedule pre-date prompts (Phase 4)
     {SYSTEM} Schedule date-day reminder

PHASE 7: MATCH LOGIC
================================================================================

7.1  {SYSTEM} After all feedback collected, run match detection:

     FULL MATCH: Both say "Interested" or "Very interested"
     → Immediate match reveal (7.2)

     SOFT MATCH: One says "Interested/Very" + other says "Maybe" **NEW**
     → Delayed notification after 48 hours (7.5)

     NO MATCH: Both "Not interested" or one "Not" + one "Maybe"
     → No action. Users can be re-matched in future groups.

     MUTUAL MAYBE: Both say "Maybe" **NEW**
     → Flag for Re-Match mechanism (Phase 10)

7.2  FULL MATCH REVEAL:
     >>NOTIFY>> to both:
     "It's a match! 🎉 You and [Name] are both interested.
      Start chatting!"

7.3  [MATCH REVEAL SCREEN]
     Full-screen celebration animation
     Shows match's photos, name, shared interests
     "You both loved [Activity]!"
     (Tap: Say Hi → opens 1-on-1 chat)

7.4  [1-ON-1 CHAT]
     Chat opens with auto-generated conversation starter: **NEW**
     "You both love [shared interest] and had a blast at [Activity].
      Pick up where you left off! 💬"

     {SYSTEM} Create Match record
     {SYSTEM} Create direct ChatRoom + ChatParticipants
     {SYSTEM} Elo update for both users

7.5  SOFT MATCH (48 hours after date): **NEW**
     >>NOTIFY>> to the "Maybe" person only:
     "Someone from your [Activity] group is interested in
      seeing you again. Want to find out who? 💫"

7.6  [SOFT MATCH SCREEN] **NEW**
     "Someone liked you! Tap to reveal."
     (Tap: Show me → Reveals the interested person's profile)
     (Tap: Not now → Notification dismissed, no action)
     --IF-- Tapped "Show me":
            [SOFT MATCH REVEAL]
            Shows the interested person's photos + name
            "Want to connect with [Name]?"
            (Tap: Yes! → Match created, chat opens with gentle starter)
            (Tap: No thanks → Nothing happens. Interested person never told.)

     Chat starter for soft matches:
     "Take it slow — no pressure. You were in the same [Activity] group.
      What stood out to you about the evening? 💛"

PHASE 8: SECOND-DATE BRIDGE
================================================================================

**NEW** — The biggest innovation. Converts matches to real dates.

8.1  WITHIN 48 HOURS OF MATCH:
     {SYSTEM} Analyze: shared activity, both users' locations,
     mutual availability (from date request data), shared interests.

     {SYSTEM} Generate second-date suggestion:
     - Activity: related to original (or from shared interests)
     - Venue: between both users' locations, highly rated
     - Time: mutual availability overlap

8.2  [SECOND-DATE SUGGESTION CARD] (appears in 1-on-1 chat)
     ┌──────────────────────────────────────┐
     │  💡 Date Idea                        │
     │                                      │
     │  [Activity] at [Venue]               │
     │  [Day], [Time]                       │
     │                                      │
     │  Why: You're both free, it's 10 min  │
     │  from both of you, and you both      │
     │  rated [original activity] 5 stars.  │
     │                                      │
     │  [Propose This Date]  [Suggest Own]  │
     └──────────────────────────────────────┘

8.3  --IF-- One person taps "Propose This Date":
     >>NOTIFY>> to the other person:
     "[Name] wants to go to [Activity] at [Venue] on [Day]!
      ✓ Accept    ✗ Suggest alternative    ⏰ Not this week"

8.4  --IF-- Accept:
     {SYSTEM} Create SecondDate record
     {SYSTEM} Set reminder for 2 hours before
     "It's a date! 🎉 [Activity] at [Venue], [Day] [Time]"

     --IF-- Suggest alternative:
     [DATE NEGOTIATION] — simple back-and-forth:
     "What works better for you?"
     (Activity picker, date/time picker, venue search)

     --IF-- Not this week:
     "No rush! We'll suggest another idea next week."

8.5  ONE WEEK AFTER MATCH (if no second date arranged):
     >>NOTIFY>>
     "Hey! You matched with [Name] last week. Don't let a great
      connection slip away — here's a fresh idea: [new suggestion]"

8.6  TWO WEEKS AFTER MATCH — CHECK-IN: **NEW**
     >>NOTIFY>>
     "How's it going with [Name]?"
     (a) We've met up again! 🎉
     (b) Still chatting, good vibes
     (c) Conversation fizzled
     (d) Prefer not to say

     {SYSTEM} Record response as conversion signal
     --IF-- (a): C₁ conversion! 🎯 Record in analytics.
     --IF-- (c): No penalty. User remains in pool for future dates.

================================================================================
PHASE 10: RE-MATCH MECHANISM
================================================================================

**NEW** For users who were in the same group and both said "Maybe"

10.1  {SYSTEM} 2 weeks after date, check for mutual "Maybe" pairs
      where neither person has blocked the other

10.2  >>NOTIFY>> to both (independently):
      "Second Look 👀: You and [Name] were in the same [Activity]
       group. Neither of you made a move — but there might be
       something there. Want to explore it?"

10.3  --IF-- BOTH tap yes (within 72 hours):
       Soft match created → chat opens
       "You both decided to take a second look!
        Sometimes the best connections are slow burns. 💛"

      --IF-- Either taps no or doesn't respond:
       Nothing happens. Neither person is notified.
       No embarrassment.
